FilmFeed

As I started shooting film in 2018, I needed a way to share my work with others. While Instagram and Flickr are some valid options, I wanted to create a more personal experience. It was also the opportunity for me to combine my 2 passions: photography & design.

Logo and branding

The logo is composed of 2 keys elements: The background is a grainy 3 colors gradient, made of a warm yellow, a vivid red and a cool blue. The combinaison creates a spectrum of colors, while the grain provides some texture on top. The logo uses the initials of FilmFeed and is a reference to the URL of the website. It is made of 2 Fs that represent the aperture number in photography.

Approche

For this personal project, I wanted it to reflect the experience of shooting film as much as possible. Simplicity and focus are two of its main components. During the entire design process, I kept asking myself if my decisions would align with this approche.
